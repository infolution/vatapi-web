VAT on e-Services

E-services supplied by non-EU businesses to private individuals in the EU i.e. business to consumer (B2C), they are taxed in the country where the customer resides.

What are e-Services

  • supply of websites or web-hosting services
  • downloaded software
  • downloaded images, text or information
  • digitised books
  • downloaded music, films or games
  • electronic auctions
  • internet service packages
